    • History

    TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for local developers and quickly evolved into an international technology conference

    Over the years, it has expanded to host thousands of attendees, featuring keynotes from industry leaders and hands-on workshops

    By 2018, TechCon partnered with major tech companies, and in 2022, it went fully hybrid, reaching a global audience.

    Mission

    TechCon’s mission is to empower innovation, promote diversity in tech, and build a connected global tech community

    We believe in providing a platform where all voices in tech can be heard, from seasoned experts to emerging talents
